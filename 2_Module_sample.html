Magic Noodle Recipe

Ingredients

  • Noodle pack
  • Chilli sauce
  • Chilli oil
  • Taste maker(if available with noodles pack)
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Peanut butter
  • Vegies as carror, cucumber, lettuce sliced as long thin strips.

For Noodles

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add in noodles from the pack.
  3. Boil noodles into it until they are 90% cooked.
  4. Remove some noodle stalk aside for later use.
  5. Strain the noodles.

For Making Magic Sauce:

  1. Take a bowl.
  2. Add taste maker if available, peanut butter, chilli oil, ketchup, soy sauce, chilli sauce to the bowl according to the spiciness you like.
  3. Add previously saved noodle stalk into it gradually to form a sticky thick paste.
  4. You can also add milk for a creamy texture.

For Garnishing

  1. Take a Servingbowl
  2. Add the boiled noodles.
  3. Pour the magic sauce onto it.
  4. Serve the top with sliced vegies or egg(optional).
  5. Sprinkle some sesame seeds on the top

Intruction

  • While eating, first mix everything in the bowl together.
  • I'll recommend to y'all to take some milk glass beside, while having noodle
